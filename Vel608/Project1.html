The youtube video I was trying to embed still didn't work. I remembered chatGPT by open AI and I gave it the prompt "I am unable to embed a youtube video in a webpage using HTML". ChatGPT solved the issue for me. in order to embed a YouTube video on a website using HTML, the video must be referred to in the following format: "https://www.youtube.com/embed/YOUTUBE_VIDEO_ID" , as opposed to the standard format for a YouTube link which is "https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YOUTUBE_VIDEO_ID". The key difference is that the "/watch?v=" portion of the standard link must be replaced with "/embed/". This format allows the video to be integrated into the website as an iframe element, rather than simply linking to the video on the YouTube website. Despite efforts, the embedded YouTube video remains of inadequate dimensions, the solution to improve the size of the embedded video eludes me.
